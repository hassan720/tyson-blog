Boxing Career

Mike Tyson is considered one of the most fearsome punchers in boxing history. During his illustrious career he amassed a sensational record of 50 victories with 44 of those wins coming by way of knockout. At age 20 years old, Mike became the youngest heavyweight champion in boxing history by knocking out Trevor Berbick in two rounds achieving the feat his trainer Cus’D’Amoto had always claimed he would. Mikes career spanned a total of 20 years and at the peak of his powers became undisputed heavyweight champion of the world. He retired in 2005 after his last Kevin McBride which he lost be 6th round knockout.
